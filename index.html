Who We Are

Helping you protect yourself

We are a team of computer scientists and security experts who want to help you build your product without letting the concerns of security weigh you down. Security flaws can destroy a small company, and tech companies have even greater risks; and the longer you wait to secure your product and your infrastructure, the easier it is for attackers to gain a foothold.

What We Do

  • We help you write a security policy for your company

  • We prioritize security concerns and perform risk vs cost analysis
